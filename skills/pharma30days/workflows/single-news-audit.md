# Workflow: Single News Audit

Use when the user pastes one article or asks whether a news item is real.

## Steps

1. Extract all claims from the article.
2. Identify company, drug, target, indication, trial, endpoint, phase, and claimed impact.
3. Search official sources for the exact asset and trial.
4. Search English media for corroboration.
5. Search Chinese sources only for context and market reaction.
6. Mark each claim as verified, partially verified, unverified, contradicted, or marketing language.
7. Map the news to catalyst type.
8. Explain stock impact separately for short, medium, and long term.

## Final answer format

- One-sentence verdict
- Claim verification table
- Asset / phase mapping
- What is new
- What is old or recycled
- Catalyst strength
- Stock impact
- Next evidence needed
