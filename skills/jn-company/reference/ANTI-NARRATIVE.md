# Anti-Narrative Filter

## AI Narrative

Do not accept "AI-enabled", "large model", "intelligent platform", "AI ecosystem" unless it changes revenue, margin, user retention, paid services, cost reduction, or product competitiveness.

Ask: Where does AI appear in the income statement? Balance sheet? Cash flow? Are customers paying for it?

## Self-Reliance Narrative

Do not accept "domestic substitution", "independent IP", "full-stack self-developed", "core technology" unless it changes supply security, product performance, gross margin, customer dependence, replacement of foreign suppliers, or bargaining power.

Ask: If foreign access is restricted, what exactly can this company still do?

## Globalization Narrative

Do not accept "going global", "overseas expansion", "international layout" unless it shows overseas revenue, profit, channels, compliance, brand, local production, or repeat customers.

Ask: Is this company exporting products, or building a real overseas operating system?

## New Quality Productive Forces Narrative

Do not accept policy wording, industrial buzzwords, conference slogans, or thematic fund language.

Ask: What scarce capability does this company actually control? Can that capability survive competition, policy change, and margin pressure?

---

# Financial Reality Check

Analyze at least:

- Revenue growth, gross margin, operating margin, net profit, free cash flow
- Debt, inventory, receivables, R&D expense, overseas revenue, capex, ROIC, valuation

Key questions:

- Is growth profitable? Is profit converting into cash?
- Is inventory rising faster than revenue? Receivables?
- Is R&D productive? Is overseas expansion improving or damaging margins?
- Is valuation already pricing in perfect execution?

---

# Valuation Stress Test

Test valuation under pressure scenarios:

- Revenue growth cut by half
- Gross margin drops by 3pp
- Overseas tariffs increase
- AI commercialization delayed by 2 years
- Domestic price war continues
- Export controls tighten
- Capex rises

Then answer: If the good story is delayed, does the stock still make sense?
