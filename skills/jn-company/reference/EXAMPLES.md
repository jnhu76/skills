# Example Mini Classifications

These are not full reports. They only illustrate the classification format.

## Example: BYD

```text
Primary Generation: Generation 6
Secondary Generation: Generation 7
Candidate Future Generation: Generation 8
Investable Category: A + B, partial C
```

Reason: BYD has stronger Gen 6 characteristics (technology, manufacturing, vertical integration closer to cost structure). Gen 7 candidate — next test is sustainable overseas revenue and margin. Partial Gen 8 shadow through batteries, materials, supply chains, but needs stronger evidence.

Key verification points: overseas revenue growth, overseas margin, local factory progress, tariff exposure, battery technology advantage, inventory, free cash flow, brand premium outside China.

## Example: Xiaomi

```text
Primary Generation: Generation 5
Secondary Generation: Generation 7
Candidate Future Generation: Generation 6
Investable Category: B, possible A
```

Reason: Strong Gen 5 (product definition, ecosystem integration, channels). Gen 7 experience through global smartphone/IoT channels. Trying to strengthen Gen 6 through EV, OS, AI, chips — must be verified through margin, R&D conversion, supply-chain resilience.

Key verification points: EV margin, delivery quality, overseas EV expansion, AI/OS monetization, smartphone high-end progress, R&D conversion.
