# Grill Me

Inspect the available conversation and material before routing.

Run `clarifying` when the intended outcome, scope boundary, or externally observable behavior cannot yet be determined from the evidence.

Run `grilling` when those are determined and a concrete plan or design exists whose premises, boundaries, or trade-offs can be challenged.

If clarification is needed, clarify first. Do not grill an unresolved intent.

If the task is already determined and no material plan or design exists to challenge, say so and stop. Do not invent a questioning session.
