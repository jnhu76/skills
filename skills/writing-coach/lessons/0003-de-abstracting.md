# Lesson 0003: De-abstracting

## Skill

Make a grand sentence concrete without making it trivial.

## Weak sentence

> AI 将重塑 RISC-V 生态，打开战略窗口。

Problem: 大词太多，现实动作太少。

## Stronger sentence

> AI Agent 可以把跨 ISA 迁移中的编译错误、依赖适配、宏替换和测试失败串成自动循环。这样一来，原来只有少数熟悉工具链的人能做的适配工作，会被更多团队尝试。

## Exercise

Rewrite this sentence:

> AI 会重塑软件生态，降低迁移成本。

Requirements:

- Do not use “生态”.
- Name at least 3 concrete actions.
- Keep it under 80 Chinese characters if possible.

## Feedback criteria

- Does it name concrete actions?
- Does it explain how cost falls?
- Does it avoid empty grandeur?
