# Lesson 0002: Logic Chain

## Skill

Connect a judgment to its consequence without jumping.

## Weak chain

```text
AI 降低门槛
→ 蓝海变红海
```

Problem: the middle is missing.

## Stronger chain

```text
AI Agent 可以处理编译、测试、日志、修复等反馈任务
→ 新领域的第一轮试错成本下降
→ 原来只有少数专家敢做的事，更多人能尝试
→ 基础适配和普通开发能力快速商品化
→ 蓝海加速变红海
```

## Exercise

Expand this sentence into a 5-step chain:

> AI Agent 会降低进入新领域的试错成本。

Use this format:

```text
1. 现象：
2. 机制：
3. 成本变化：
4. 参与者变化：
5. 后果：
```

## Feedback criteria

- Does each step follow from the previous one?
- Is there a mechanism step?
- Does the chain explain who enters and what becomes cheap?
