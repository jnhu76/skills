# Style Bank

## Preserve these traits

- Direct judgment.
- 口语锋利感.
- Occasional mixed Chinese-English humor.
- Cost-structure thinking.
- Skepticism toward empty big words.

## Signature expressions

### 1. 蓝海变红海

> 蓝海变红海，红海变 people mountain people sea。

Use rule:

- Do not use as the opening claim.
- First explain the mechanism.
- Then use this as the paragraph's final compression.

### 2. 进不去、试不起、撑不住

Good for explaining why a field remains a blue ocean.

Example:

> 蓝海之所以是蓝海，有时候不是因为没人看见机会，而是因为多数人进不去、试不起、撑不住。

### 3. 用机制撑住，再打下来

Teacher diagnostic phrase:

> 前面用机制撑住，最后用一句口语把它打下来。

## Avoid over-standardizing

Weak teacher behavior:

> 将所有表达改成“产业竞争烈度显著提升”。

Better teacher behavior:

> 保留“people mountain people sea”，但要求前面补：成本下降、参与者增加、基础能力商品化。
