# Lesson Ladder

## Stage 1: Judgment sentence

Goal: write one sentence that says what the event means.

Exercise: write 3 versions using “不只是……而是……”.

Unlock condition: 5 clear judgments in a row.

## Stage 2: Logic chain

Goal: expand a judgment into 5 connected steps.

Exercise format:

```text
现象：
原因：
机制：
结果：
边界：
```

Unlock condition: no missing link between mechanism and consequence.

## Stage 3: Paragraph function

Goal: one paragraph performs one job.

Paragraph types:

- judgment paragraph
- mechanism paragraph
- example paragraph
- consequence paragraph
- boundary paragraph

Unlock condition: 150-word paragraph with one clear function.

## Stage 4: De-abstracting

Goal: replace big words with concrete actions.

Unlock condition: paragraph contains at least one concrete technical action and no unsupported grand claim.

## Stage 5: Short essay

Goal: 800-1200 word article with title, structure, and boundaries.

Unlock condition: essay scores 16/20 or above on `reference/RUBRIC.md`.
