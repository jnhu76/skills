# Mission: Technology-Analysis Writing

## Why

I want to learn how to write clear Chinese technology-analysis essays and comments.

My goal is not literary prose. I want to express technical and industry judgments clearly, especially around AI Agent, RISC-V, software engineering, chip development, open source, and cost-structure changes.

The writing should feel grounded, not grandiose. It should preserve my personal 口语锋利感 while making the logic chain clear.

## Success looks like

- I can turn a raw thought into one clear core judgment.
- I can expand a judgment into a complete logic chain.
- I can explain mechanisms instead of only using abstract words.
- I can revise a paragraph after feedback and make it less empty.
- I can write an 800-1200 word technology-analysis essay with clear structure, examples, boundaries, and personal voice.
- I can preserve expressions like “蓝海变红海，红海变 people mountain people sea” by placing them after enough reasoning.

## Constraints

- Prefer short exercises and frequent feedback.
- Do not over-polish into report style.
- Do not remove personal voice.
- Focus on Chinese writing first.
- Use Markdown files as long-term memory.
- The teacher should act like a real writing coach: diagnose, assign, review, track progress, and push for second drafts.

## Out of scope

- Literary fiction writing.
- Academic paper writing.
- Marketing copywriting.
- Pure grammar drills without connection to real writing.
